Buying a house? Here's an interesting peek into London's area-wise Real Estate Market Pricing for Investment decisions

This project aims to provide pricing insights into London's vibrant Real Estate Residential Property market, from an Investor's point of view, so as to be able to invest in places were the housing prices are low and the facilities (shops,restaurants,parks,Hotels, etc.) and social venues are nearby. It makes use of Carto's API to fetch geojson data for London Boroughs.

Environmental, Local, and Societal Consequences of Uranium Mining in Jadugora, Jharkhand, India

A case study focused on analyzing the environmental, local, and societal consequences of uranium mining in Jadugora, Jharkhand, India. The project uses advanced Data science and machine learning techniques to assess the relationships between mining activities and various impact indicators, including environmental contamination, health outcomes, and socioeconomic factors in context of the communities surrounding the mines.
